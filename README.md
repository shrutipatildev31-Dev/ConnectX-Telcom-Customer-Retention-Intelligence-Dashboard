### **# Telecom Customer Churn Intelligence Dashboard**



#### **## Project Overview**



Customer churn is one of the biggest challenges in the telecom industry. Acquiring new customers is significantly more expensive than retaining existing ones, making churn analysis a critical business function.



This project analyzes customer behavior, churn drivers, retention patterns, service adoption, and revenue impact using SQL and Power BI. The dashboard transforms raw telecom customer data into actionable business insights that support customer retention and revenue growth strategies.



\---



#### **## Business Objective**



The primary objective of this project is to answer the following business questions:



\* Which customer segments are most likely to churn?

\* What are the major reasons customers leave?

\* How does customer tenure influence churn risk?

\* Which contract types have the highest retention?

\* How do service subscriptions affect customer loyalty?

\* Which geographic regions generate the most revenue?

\* Which payment methods contribute the most revenue?

\* How much revenue is lost because of customer churn?

Business Question:

Which age groups experience the highest churn risk across different tenure periods?

Senior customers within their first 12 months show the highest churn risk. Churn decreases significantly as customer tenure increases.

\### Visual 2: Top 5 Drivers of Customer Churn

What are the primary reasons customers leave?

###### \# 3. Revenue Analysis



\### KPIs



\* Total Revenue: $21.4M

\* Revenue Lost: $4M

\* ARPU: $3K

\* Revenue Retention: 83%

\* Monthly Charge: 448K



\### Visual 1: Revenue Loss Distribution



Business Question:

Which tenure segments contribute most to revenue loss?



Key Insight:

Customers with more than 2 years of tenure account for the majority of revenue loss due to their higher customer value.



\---



\### Visual 2: Top 10 Revenue-Generating Cities



Business Question:

Which geographic areas generate the highest revenue?



Key Insight:

Revenue is concentrated within major California metropolitan regions including Los Angeles, San Diego, and the Bay Area.



\---



\### Visual 3: Revenue Contribution by Payment Method



Business Question:

Which payment methods contribute the most revenue?



Key Insight:

Bank Withdrawal and Credit Card payments generate the highest revenue contribution, while mailed checks contribute minimally.

#### **## Key Business Findings**



\### Customer Retention



\* Long-term contracts significantly improve retention.

\* Month-to-Month customers are the highest churn-risk segment.

\* Service adoption positively impacts customer loyalty.



\### Churn Drivers



\* Competitor offers are a leading cause of churn.

\* High pricing and service dissatisfaction accelerate customer loss.

\* Early-tenure customers require proactive retention efforts.



\### Revenue



\* Revenue loss is concentrated among high-value customer segments.

\* Revenue generation is geographically concentrated.

\* Digital payment methods dominate revenue contribution.
